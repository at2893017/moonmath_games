Apollo 11

Objectives of Apollo 11

The primary goal of Apollo 11, set by John F. Kennedy, the president of the United States in 1961, was to perform a landing on the surface of the moon and sucessfully make it back to Earth. They also wanted to place television cameras for scientists and people all around the world to see the moon. The astronauts would also gather samples of moon to return for further study in Earth. They were able to succesfully commplete all these goals and the three astronauts made it back July 24, 1969!
